# Advanced Mindfulness and Grounding
Mindfulness is the practice of being present without judgment.
1. **The Body Scan:** Slowly mentaly scan from your toes to your head, noticing any tension or sensation without trying to change it.
2. **Thought Labeling:** When a thought arises, label it ("Thinking," "Worrying," "Planning") and let it float away like a cloud.
3. **Leaves on a Stream:** Imagine you are sitting by a stream. Place every difficult thought on a leaf and watch it float out of sight.
4. **Sensory Anchoring:** Pick one sense (like hearing) and see how many distinct sounds you can identify in 60 seconds.
