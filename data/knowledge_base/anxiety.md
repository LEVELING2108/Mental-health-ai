# Coping with Anxiety
Anxiety can feel overwhelming, but there are proven techniques to manage it in the moment.
1. **The 5-4-3-2-1 Technique:** Identify 5 things you see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you hear, 2 things you can smell, and 1 thing you can taste. This grounding exercise shifts your focus from internal panic to the external world.
2. **Diaphragmatic Breathing:** Place one hand on your chest and the other on your belly. Inhale slowly through your nose so your belly moves out against your hand. Exhale through pursed lips.
3. **Limit Stimulants:** Reducing caffeine and nicotine can help lower your baseline physical anxiety symptoms.
