# Cultivating Self-Esteem
Your value is inherent and not dependent on your achievements or others' opinions.
1. **The Self-Compassion Break:** When you're struggling, say to yourself: "This is a moment of suffering. Suffering is part of life. May I be kind to myself in this moment."
2. **Challenge the Inner Critic:** When you have a negative thought, ask: "Would I say this to a dear friend?" If not, don't say it to yourself.
3. **Core Values Alignment:** Identify three things that truly matter to you (e.g., kindness, creativity, honesty). Focus on acting according to these values rather than seeking external validation.
4. **Celebration of Small Wins:** Acknowledge one small thing you did well today, even if it's just "I got dressed."
