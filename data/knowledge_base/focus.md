# Improving Focus and Managing ADHD-like Symptoms
When your brain feels scattered, structure is your best friend.
1. **The Pomodoro Technique:** Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. This uses the brain's natural focus cycles.
2. **Body Doubling:** If you're struggling to start a task, try doing it while someone else is in the room or on a video call (even if they are working on something else).
3. **The "Two-Minute Rule":** If a task takes less than two minutes, do it immediately. Don't add it to a list.
4. **Environment Design:** Clear your physical workspace of anything not related to your current task to reduce "visual noise."
