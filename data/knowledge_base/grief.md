# Navigating Grief and Loss
Grief is a natural response to loss, and it doesn't follow a straight line.
1. **Allow the Waves:** Understand that grief comes in waves. Some days will be harder than others; allow yourself to feel without judgment.
2. **Social Support:** Reach out to friends or support groups. Sharing your story can be a powerful part of the healing process.
3. **Routine Matters:** Maintaining a basic routine (regular meals, sleep, hygiene) provides a sense of stability during chaotic emotional times.
4. **Gentle Self-Care:** Avoid making major life decisions immediately after a significant loss. Be patient with your healing.
