# Managing Burnout and Workplace Stress
Burnout is a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by excessive and prolonged stress.
1. **Set Boundaries:** Define clear start and end times for your workday. Avoid checking emails during personal time.
2. **Take Micro-breaks:** Every hour, take a 5-minute break away from your screen to stretch or hydrate.
3. **Prioritize Tasks:** Use the Eisenhower Matrix to focus on what is truly important rather than just urgent.
4. **Learn to Say No:** Protect your energy by not over-committing to projects that exceed your capacity.
