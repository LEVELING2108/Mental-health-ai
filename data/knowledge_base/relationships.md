# Building Healthy Relationships
Healthy relationships are built on trust, communication, and mutual respect.
1. **The "I" Statement Technique:** Instead of saying "You make me angry," try "I feel hurt when this happens." This reduces defensiveness.
2. **Active Listening:** Truly hear the other person without preparing your rebuttal while they speak. Reflect back what you heard: "It sounds like you're feeling..."
3. **Emotional Boundaries:** Understand where you end and the other person begins. You are responsible for your own emotions, not theirs.
4. **The 5:1 Ratio:** Healthy relationships typically have five positive interactions for every one negative interaction.
