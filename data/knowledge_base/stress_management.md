# Stress Management Techniques
Chronic stress can impact both your physical and mental health. Managing it effectively is key to long-term well-being.

### Effective Stress Reducers:
1. **The "Stop, Breathe, Reflect" Method:** When you feel stress rising, stop what you are doing, take three deep breaths, and reflect on what specifically is triggering the stress.
2. **Prioritization (Eisenhower Matrix):** Divide your tasks into:
   - Urgent and Important
   - Not Urgent but Important
   - Urgent but Not Important
   - Not Urgent and Not Important
   Focus on the Important tasks first to reduce the feeling of being overwhelmed.
3. **Physical Relaxation:** Try Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR). Tense and then release each muscle group in your body, starting from your toes and moving up to your head.
4. **Time Buffers:** Avoid back-to-back scheduling. Give yourself 10-15 minutes between tasks or meetings to reset.
5. **Nature Exposure:** Spending even 20 minutes in a green space has been shown to significantly lower cortisol (stress hormone) levels.
