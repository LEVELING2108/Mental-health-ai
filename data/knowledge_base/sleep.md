# Improving Sleep Hygiene
Quality sleep is fundamental to mental health stability.
1. **Consistency:** Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day, even on weekends.
2. **The 60-Minute Rule:** Stop using electronic devices (phones, laptops) 60 minutes before bed to reduce blue light exposure.
3. **Environment:** Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool (around 18°C or 65°F).
4. **Worry Time:** If racing thoughts keep you awake, try a "brain dump"—write down everything on your mind 2 hours before bed so your brain feels "cleared."
